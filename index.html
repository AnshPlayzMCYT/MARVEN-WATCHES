Watch Care

If you wear your automatic watch daily, your natural movement will keep it wound. If you don't wear it for a few days, it may stop. Simply wind it manually via the crown (20-30 turns) and set the time. Avoid changing the date between 9 PM and 3 AM, as this can damage the movement.

Please check the water resistance (WR) rating for your specific model. 50M (5 ATM) is suitable for splashes and light rain. 100M (10 ATM) is suitable for swimming. 200M+ (20 ATM) is suitable for diving. Always ensure the crown is fully screwed down before exposure to water.

The Marven Warranty

The 'Marven Promise' is our commitment to quality and to you. We stand by the craftsmanship of every timepiece we create. All Marven watches are covered by a 3-Year International Warranty from the date of purchase.

What Is Covered?

Our warranty covers manufacturing and mechanical defects. This includes:

  • The watch movement (gaining/losing significant time, stopping).
  • Manufacturing defects on the case, dial, hands, or crystal.

What Is Not Covered?

The warranty does not cover issues arising from normal use, accidents, or improper handling. This includes:

  • Normal wear and tear (e.g., scratches on the case, bracelet, or crystal).
  • Wear or damage to the strap (leather, rubber, etc.).
  • Damage caused by accidents, impact, or mishandling.
  • Water damage resulting from failure to secure the crown or exceeding the watch's stated water resistance.
  • Battery replacement for quartz models.

How to Obtain Service

If you believe your watch requires service under warranty, please contact our support team at support@marvenwatches.com with your order details and a description of the issue. We will guide you through the process of sending your watch to our authorized service center for inspection and repair.
